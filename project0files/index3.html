Intent

Your favorite casual raiding guild on Faerlina

Intent is a WoW classic guild located on the server Faerlina. This guild is the perfect balance of social and serious. Whether you enjoy hardcore raiding or just chatting with guildies, this is the guild for you. Browse this page to learn more about the raid teams, requirements, and members.

Raiders are required to bring the following items to raid:
  1. Greater Fire Protection Potion x5
  2. Greater Arcane Protection Potion x3
  3. Major Mana Potion/Major Healing Potion x10
  4. Class Specific Consumables
  5. Onyxia Scale Cloak (BWL)
